Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · B-easy by B-easy team

1. Overview

B-easy ("the App") is a business inventory and billing application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The vast majority of your data never leaves your device. The following is stored only on your iPhone or iPad in an encrypted local database:

  • Inventory items, stock levels, and product photos
  • Sales and purchase transactions
  • Customer and supplier profiles (including GSTIN)
  • Invoices, GST reports, and financial summaries
  • App settings and preferences
We cannot access, read, or recover this data. It exists only on your device. If you delete the app without backing up, this data is permanently lost.

3. Data Stored on Our Servers

We use Supabase (a secure, open-source backend) to store the following minimal data for authentication purposes only:

  • Phone number — used for OTP-based sign-in
  • Your name and shop name — provided during sign-up
  • Account creation date

This data is stored securely with Row Level Security (RLS), meaning only you can access your own profile.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — Authentication and user profile storage. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Twilio — SMS delivery for OTP verification. Twilio Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini API — AI-powered voice entry and bill scanning. Text prompts (item names, quantities, prices) are sent to Google's servers for processing. No personal information, financial records, or customer data is included in these prompts. Google AI Terms

5. Permissions We Request

  • Camera — To capture product photos and scan barcodes
  • Microphone — For voice-based sales and purchase entry
  • Speech Recognition — To transcribe voice input into text
  • Photo Library — To select product images from your gallery

These permissions are used solely for the stated purposes and can be revoked at any time in your device Settings.

6. Data Backup

You can export your local database as a backup file using the in-app "Data Backup" feature. This file is controlled entirely by you — we never upload or access it. You can restore from a backup on the same or a different device.

7. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This will:

  • Delete your Supabase authentication record and profile
  • Erase all local data from your device
  • This action is permanent and irreversible

8. Children's Privacy

B-easy is a business application not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
